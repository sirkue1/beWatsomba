Our Story

Founded in 2015, Serenity Wellness Center was born from a simple belief: everyone deserves access to holistic healing and transformative wellness experiences. What started as a small practice has grown into a sanctuary for those seeking refuge from the hustle of modern life.

Our founder, Dr. Maya Chen, combines her background in traditional Chinese medicine with Western holistic practices to create a unique approach to wellness that honors both ancient wisdom and modern science.
